About

The person behind the page

Vadodara, Gujarat  ·  2025

Someone who came to management through a curiosity about why people make the decisions they do.

Whether it’s a consumer choosing a product, an investor backing a founder, or a country setting policy — the question of human judgment has always fascinated me.

I write to think. Most of my essays start as questions I can’t shake, and end as something I didn’t expect. My book reviews are less summaries and more arguments with the author.

I’m headed to VGSOM, IIT Kharagpur for my MBA, where I hope to bring these ideas into strategy, consulting, or wherever the most interesting problems live.
